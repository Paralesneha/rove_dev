Visa Overview

The Greek Permanent Residence, with an investment of €250.000 in Real Estate, is Europe's most affordable residence program. This investment covers all non-EU nationals and their immediate family members and gives them the right to obtain European citizenship.

Country Overview

Greece is the most beautiful country in the world, strategically located at the crossroads of Europe, Asia, and Africa. It is considered the cradle of Western civilization. Greece has long been a popular tourist destination for its breathtaking locations, hospitable people, stunning beaches, fantastic weather and unique flavours. It is a democratic country with a developed economy that offers a high standard and safety of living yet affordable.

Important Announcements

As of August 1st, 2023, the investment amount for the Greek Golden Visa property acquisition route will be increased from €250,000 to €500,000. This new regulation will apply to the most desired real estate zones, i.e., the greater Athens area, the municipality of Thessaloniki, Mykonos, and Santorini.
In the regions where the new regulations apply, an applicant must invest €500,000 in ONE (1) property only. In other areas where the new regulation is not in force, the investment can be made in more than one property with a total amount of at least €250,000.
A down payment of 10% of the property value before July 31st, 2023, entitles the investor to still fall under the €250,000 scheme, provided the contract is concluded by December 31st, 2023.

Requirements

  • Applicant must be at least 18 years of age at the time of investment.
  • A minimum investment of €250,000 in Greece (until 31st July 2023)
  • Private health insurance policy from an agency located in Greece
